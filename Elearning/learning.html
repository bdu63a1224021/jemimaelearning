Finland in Northern-European Nation bordering Sweden, Norway& Russia. Its Capital is Helsinki. Finland is 8th Expensive Country in Europe. And yes, it ranks #1 globally in Education



Education in Finland is mostly free of cost. Mostly , all the schools and universities are handled by Government. If it you are likely to join in private universities for higher Education , Finland Goverment will offers you Scholarships too



• No Homeworks
• Schooling starts at the age of 7 years & 9 years of Compulasary Education.
• School timings are from 9am onwards to 2:30pm
•No exams
•The only exam in Higher Education is Matriculation Exam at the end of the Schooling , which can be used for applying in Universities for higher Education.



Teachers should complete Master's Degree for primary Education.
• For High School Education , teachers must have PhD .
